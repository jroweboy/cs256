William "scarra" Lee

Age: 25
Years Played: 5

In early November 2010, scarra helped form Rock Solid along with Naryt, Rambo, Voyboy, Araragi, samtheham, and Th3RaT. With that lineup, scarra was able to place third at the Newegg Winter Wanfest 2010.

On September 22, 2011, scarra and Rock Solid were picked up by Team Dignitas. Team Dignitas was looking for a new League of Legends team to sponsor after disbanding of its European-based team. Now having financial sponsorship, scarra was able to attend IEM Season VI - Global Challenge New York, where his team took fifth/sixth place, taking one game off of Team SoloMid.

October 31st, Scarra was named Head Coach of Counter Logic Gaming.
